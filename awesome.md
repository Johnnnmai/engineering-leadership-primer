# Awesome Engineering Management

Curated collection of resources for engineering management: practitioner stories, leadership guides, company engineering blogs, books, courses, and case studies.

This is the list of resources I used while researching content for the [EM Interview Field Guide](README.md). The collection turned out to be so useful that I decided to publish it separately.

## Books

Essential reading for engineering managers:

- Julie Zhuo: The Making of a Manager (2019) - the definitive book for new engineering managers
- Camille Fournier: The Manager's Path (2017) - technical management from IC to CTO
- Will Larson: An Elegant Puzzle (2019) - systems of engineering management
- Lara Hogan: Resilient Management (2019) - people-first engineering leadership
- Kim Scott: Radical Candor (2017) - direct feedback and caring personally
- Michael Lopp: Managing Humans (2016) - practical engineering management wisdom
- Patrick Lencioni: The Five Dysfunctions of a Team (2002) - team dynamics framework
- Gene Kim et al.: The Phoenix Project (2013) - DevOps and engineering transformation
- Marty Cagan: Empowered (2020) - how the best companies build product teams
- Andrew Grove: High Output Management (1983) - the original engineering management playbook
