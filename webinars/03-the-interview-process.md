## Description

The EM hiring landscape blends people leadership assessment with technical evaluation. By examining real candidate experiences from Reddit and X, this session prepares participants for the EM screening reality.

For a comprehensive breakdown of EM interview formats, question banks, and preparation strategies, see [The 0→1 EM Interview Playbook](https://valenx.org/books/em) - chapters on interview process and management case study execution cover these topics with worked examples.

## Topics Covered

- Interview process structure - the 5-7 typical steps from recruiter screen to final round, based on 980+ job descriptions and candidate reports
- People management questions - hiring, coaching, performance management, conflict resolution, retention
- Technical strategy rounds - architecture decisions, tech debt management, system design at the EM level
- Behavioral interviews - preparing stories using company values (Amazon leadership principles as a template)
- Management case studies - preview of scenario-based assessments (covered in detail in webinar 4)
- System design for managers - how EM system design differs from IC system design

## Key Findings

### Interview Process Structure

From job descriptions and candidate reports, the typical EM interview has 5-7 steps (not all companies use all of them):

1. CV screening
2. Recruiter call - who are you, management experience, team size, salary expectations
3. Hiring manager interview - management philosophy, team-building experience, leadership stories
4. People management round - coaching scenarios, performance management, conflict resolution
5. Technical strategy/system design - architecture at the leadership level, tech debt prioritization
6. Behavioral/leadership - values alignment, cross-functional influence, difficult decisions
7. Executive/skip-level interview - alignment with organizational direction
