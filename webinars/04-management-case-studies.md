# Webinar 4: Management Case Studies

- Date: March 9, 2026
- Host: [Alexey Grigorev](https://www.linkedin.com/in/agrigorev/)
- [Maven](https://maven.com/p/250595/ai-engineering-take-home-assignments)

## Description

Management case studies are where your ability to analyze complex leadership scenarios and make sound decisions is truly measured. This session focuses on moving beyond management theory to mastering execution in scenario-based assessments used by top-tier companies.

## Topics Covered

1. Case study analysis - examining recent Q4 2025 and Q1 2026 EM interview scenarios to understand current hiring standards
2. Scenario walkthrough - deconstructing real-world management cases (team turnarounds, reorgs, incident response, delivery recovery) and analyzing effective approaches with trade-offs
3. End-to-end management analysis - building a complete response for management scenario questions, emphasizing structured thinking, stakeholder awareness, and people-centered decision-making

Practice resource: [The 0→1 EM Interview Playbook](https://valenx.org/books/em) includes a full chapter on management case study execution with real examples, evaluation rubrics, and common pitfalls that cost candidates offers.
